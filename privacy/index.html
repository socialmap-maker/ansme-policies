🔒 AnsMe Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2025

📊 Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, username, profile details
  • Content: Letters and messages you create
  • Location Data: Used only for map features (with your permission)
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous app performance data

🎯 How We Use Your Data

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Show nearby letters and enable location-based features
  • Enable messaging between users
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Send important app updates (not marketing)

🛡️ Data Protection & Security

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted using AES encryption
  • Secure Storage: We use hardware-level keychain security
  • Rate Limiting: Advanced protection against spam and abuse
  • Edge Functions: Server-side validation for all sensitive operations
  • Input Sanitization: All user inputs are validated and sanitized

🗺️ Location Data

  • Used only for map features and distance calculations
  • Not tracked when app is closed
  • You can disable location access anytime in device settings
  • Location data is not shared with third parties

👥 Data Sharing

  • Public: Profile info and letters you choose to share
  • Private: Direct messages are private between participants
  • Third Parties: We do not sell or rent personal data
  • Service Providers: Only essential infrastructure providers (Supabase for backend)

🧒 Children's Privacy (Under 13)

Our app is designed for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

🔧 Your Rights

  • Access: View your data through the app
  • Delete: Delete your account and all data
  • Correct: Update your profile information
  • Export: Contact us for data export

📧 Contact Us

For privacy-related questions: ansmeappofficial@gmail.com

📱 Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Backend infrastructure and database
  • OpenStreetMap: Map tiles and location search
  • React Native: Mobile app framework

🔄 Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify users of significant changes through the app.

⚖️ Legal Basis

We process your data based on:

  • Your consent (for location and optional features)
  • Contract performance (to provide app services)
  • Legitimate interest (for app improvement and security)

This privacy policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play requirements.
