🗑️ AnsMe Account Deletion

Last updated: June 23, 2025

⚠️ Important Warning

Account deletion is irreversible! This action permanently deletes all your data and makes it impossible to recover your account.

🗂️ Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • ✅ Your profile information (username, email, profile picture)
  • ✅ All letters and messages you've written
  • ✅ Direct messages sent and received
  • ✅ Account settings and preferences
  • ✅ In-app purchase history
  • ✅ Location history and map interactions
  • ✅ Login tokens and session data

📋 Account Deletion Steps

Method 1: Through the App

  1. Open the AnsMe app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Tap the Settings button
  4. Navigate to "Account & Security" section
  5. Tap "Delete My Account" button
  6. Enter your password and confirm the action
  7. Tap "Permanently Delete Account" button

Method 2: Via Email

If you cannot access the app or are experiencing technical issues:

  1. 📧 Send an email to ansmeappofficial@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request - AnsMe"
  3. In the message, please include:
    • Your account email address
    • Your username
    • Reason for deletion (optional)

⏱️ Processing Time

  • In-app: Processed immediately (within 30 seconds)
  • Via email: Processed within 24-48 hours
  • Confirmation: Email notification when process is complete

🔄 After Processing

30-Day Retention Period

For security and legal compliance reasons:

  • Your data will be securely archived for 30 days
  • You cannot recover your account during this period
  • After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted
  • This process is completely automated

📊 Retained Data

Due to legal requirements, the following data may be retained for a limited time:

  • 💰 Financial records: 7 years (tax law requirement)
  • 🔒 Security logs: 90 days (for abuse detection)
  • 📊 Anonymous statistics: Without personal identifiers

🆘 Help and Support

For questions about account deletion:

  • 📧 Email: ansmeappofficial@gmail.com
  • ⏰ Response time: Within 24 hours
  • 🌍 Languages: English and Turkish support

⚖️ Legal Rights

This account deletion policy complies with:

  • GDPR - European Union Data Protection Regulation
  • CCPA - California Consumer Privacy Act
  • KVKK - Turkey Personal Data Protection Law
  • Google Play - Developer Policy Center

This policy is prepared for full compliance with Google Play Store account deletion requirements.
